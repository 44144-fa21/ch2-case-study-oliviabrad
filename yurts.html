Enjoy Nature in Luxury

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the groud. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilites are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
